Basic Signals

Signals are a new way to manage state in Angular applications. They provide a more declarative and reactive approach to state management, making it easier to reason about your application's state and how it changes over time.

1. Writable signals - Writable signals provide an API for updating their values directly. Signals may be either writable or read-only.
